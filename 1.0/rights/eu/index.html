This vocabulary provides concepts representing the EU Fundamental Rights for use with Data Privacy Vocabulary (DPV) Specification to provide additional concepts regarding DataSubjectRights.

The namespace for terms in RIGHTS-EU is https://w3id.org/dpv/rights/eu#
The suggested prefix for the namespace is rights-eu
The RIGHTS-EU vocabulary and its documentation is available on GitHub.

Issue 75: WARNING: Unstable concepts rightsWIPhelp-wantedeu-rights

The https://w3id.org/dpv/rights/eu document provides a taxonomy of EU Charter of Fundamental Rights. The EUROVOC thesauri/taxonomies also provide concepts on these, e.g. https://op.europa.eu/en/web/eu-vocabularies/concept/-/resource?uri=http://eurovoc.europa.eu/6286 and https://op.europa.eu/en/web/eu-vocabularies/concept/-/resource?uri=http://eurovoc.europa.eu/538

DPV should either reuse these, or align with this. I'm unsure how to interpret these, extract them as a dataset, or how they are structured in relation to laws/documents, or being declared as instances of Right.
