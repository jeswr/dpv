DPVS-PD extends the DPV-SKOS: Data Privacy Vocabulary serialised in RDFS & SKOS to provide categories of personal data. The canonical URL for DPVO-PD is https://w3id.org/dpv-owl/dpv-pd which contains (this) specification. The namespace for DPV terms is https://w3id.org/dpv/dpv-owl/dpv-pd#, the suggested prefix is dpvo-pd, and this document along with source and releases are available at https://github.com/w3c/dpv.

Issue 2: express existence and relations to other vocabularies

DPV's list of concepts is not universal nor exhaustive. It also is opinionated in that there are other vocabularies with different perspectives on categorisation of personal data concepts. This section should list such other external vocabularies, and how they relate to DPV's PD extension for personal data categories.
