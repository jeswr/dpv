risk consequence taxonomy

<ghurlbot> Issue 163 [Review] EU AI Act Concepts (by DelaramGlp)

delaram pointed out that 'Misuse' can be a risk or risk source but is currently indicated as a consequence

currently, we have consequence and impact taxonomy which is opinionated. However, the same 'consequence' can be a risk or risk source. So how to enable this flexibility? Suggestion: 1) Event as top concept and then adopter chooses events to be expressed as risk sources or risks or impacts etc; 2) Risk as top concept and adopter chooses the risk (event) and expresses it as source or impact etc.

discussed that having 'risk' as the top concept might be a better solution and then let the use-case specify whether the risk is a risk source or a consequence or an impact

we can categorise risks as 'risk of consequence' and 'risk of impact' to help with the taxonomy
